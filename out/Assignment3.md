#### Exercise `subject_expansion`

and conversely that `M` _expands_ to `N` if `N —→ M`.

`M —→ N` and `∅ ⊢ N ⦂ A` imply `∅ ⊢ M ⦂ A`.

with case expressions and one not involving case expressions.
